THE PROBLEM

The Daily Struggle for Good Food is Real.

For students and busy professionals, finding affordable, nutritious, and homely food can feel like a full-time job. The traditional tiffin system is often chaotic—requiring daily calls to check menus, manual tracking of meals, and uncertainty about your next delivery. For tiffin providers, managing customer records by hand is inefficient and prone to errors, limiting their ability to grow.

OUR SOLUTION

A Seamless Tiffin Experience for Everyone.

For Customers

Effortlessly find and subscribe to tiffin services in your area. With TiffinEZ, you can view daily menus, track your remaining tiffin balance in real-time, manage your orders, and even scan a QR code for a quick dine-in experience. Your next delicious, home-cooked meal is just a tap away.

For Owners

Digitize and grow your tiffin business. Our platform gives you a powerful admin dashboard to manage customers, update menus instantly, track daily orders, and gain valuable business insights. Spend less time on manual record-keeping and more time delighting your customers.

For Delivery Partners

Make deliveries smarter and faster. Our app provides an automatically optimized route for all your daily orders, ensuring you can deliver food efficiently. This means less time on the road, happier customers, and fresher meals, every time.

ABOUT

We power reliable, homely meals with modern software.

TiffinEZ is a digital platform by NourishX Innovations that connects customers with trusted local tiffin providers and equips operators with the software they need to run efficiently. We solve discovery, ordering, menu visibility, and delivery coordination for customers, while replacing manual, error-prone processes for tiffin businesses with a simple dashboard and delivery tooling. Our primary audience includes students and working professionals seeking affordable, consistent, home-style food, and local operators who want to modernize and grow.

PRODUCT

Customer App

Find nearby tiffins, view daily menus, subscribe to plans, manage orders, and track remaining meals. Optimized for quick reorders and QR-based dine-in.

Owner Dashboard

Update menus in seconds, manage customers and subscriptions, track orders and balances, and view basic analytics—no spreadsheets required.

Delivery Companion

Automatically sequenced routes and order lists help partners complete rounds faster with fewer errors and fresher deliveries.

Current Stage: Private development with live demos; mobile apps in active build. Public waitlist open via ceo@tiffinez.in.
