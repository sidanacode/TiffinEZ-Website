ABOUT US

Digital-native platform for the tiffin ecosystem

TiffinEZ is a digital-native food-tech startup building a unified platform for customers, providers, and delivery partners. Operated by NourishX Innovation LLP (DPIIT-recognized) and headquartered in India, our mission is to make access to reliable, homestyle food effortless and help local providers grow with software that’s simple, fast, and scalable.

The Problem

Customers face fragmented discovery, inconsistent menus, manual tracking of remaining meals, and unpredictable delivery. Providers rely on pen-and-paper workflows that cause errors in subscriptions and balances, lack real-time menu updates, and limit insight. Delivery partners struggle with inefficient routes and delays.

Our Solution

A connected platform—Customer App, Provider Dashboard, and Delivery Companion—creates transparency, predictability, and efficiency across the journey.

Current Stage

Private pilot with live demos. Mobile apps in active development. Join the waitlist: ceo@tiffinez.in.

Founding Story

TiffinEZ started when our team experienced firsthand how difficult it was to consistently access homestyle food while studying and working across different cities. We spoke with local tiffin providers who were maintaining ledgers by hand and fielding dozens of calls daily for menu updates and delivery clarifications. We began prototyping lightweight tools to digitize the day-to-day—from subscription tracking to delivery sequencing—which evolved into a connected platform focused on reliability for customers and operational clarity for providers.

Vision & Mission

Vision: Make access to nutritious, homestyle meals as effortless as turning on a tap.
Mission: Build simple, fast software that empowers local tiffin providers to grow sustainably while delivering trusted, affordable meals to customers.

Advisors & Partners
